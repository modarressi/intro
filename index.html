I am an Associate Professor of Computer Enginenring at University of Tehran, in the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering.
As of March 2025, I serve as the head of the Department of Computer Architecture of the school.

I also lead the Intelligent Architectures for Computing Sytems research group. My current research is focused in the area of hardware acceleration for AI, parallel architectures and networks-on-chip, and modern memory systems. (publications)

I received my PhD in Computer Engineering from Sharif University of Technology, Tehran, Iran, in 2011. Throughout my career, I have had the privilege of undertaking several wonderful research visits, including EPFL in Switzerland (2009-2010), Malardalen University (MDU) in Sweden (2018), and TU Dresden in Germany (2019).

My homepage on the School of ECE's website may contain some news updates.

Research

My current research projects include:
    -AI acceleration on FPGA for LLMs, video processing, and healthcare
    -Emerging memory systems and processing-in-memory
    -Edge AI for IoT systems

In recent years, we have expanded our research through international collaborations, particularly with the Heterogeneous Systems Research Group at Malardalen University, Sweden. This partnership has resulted in the publication of over 30 joint papers.
